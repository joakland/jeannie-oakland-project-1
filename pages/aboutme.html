Jeannie Oakland

Weyt-kp xwexwéytep! Hello everyone! I'm Jeannie Oakland, a Secwepemc, First Nations woman with a strong passion for the great outdoors.Currently residing in the harbour city, Nanaimo, BC. On the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw. I moved to Nanaimo in 2022 and I love it. It was a huge change from living in the interior of BC most of my life. It took some time to adapt but the only thing I really miss is back roads. Most of the back roads on the Island are gated and controlled. I love exploring whether I'm out in my truck, kayaking, hiking or sailing. Seeing new places and experiencing new things is something I always seem to crave.
